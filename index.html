Empower small business, secure big future

We are a passionate team of three co-founders sponsored by the Wales Cyber Innovation Hub and the Welsh government. Our collaboration stems from a shared empathy for the injustice faced by smaller organisations when it comes to current cybersecurity solutions being too expensive and technically complex. Motivated by this, we are committed to developing a risk-appropriate, simple, accessible, and affordable all-in-one platform aimed at spreading impactful security measures to SMEs.

Bridge existing gaps, build new bridges

To establish our credibility, we are grounding ourselves with the Cyber Essentials, a government-backed scheme designed to protect organisations from cyber-attacks. While valuable, its technical nature can be daunting for those without expertise. By assessing your organisation's needs and automating technical checks, we simplify the entire journey with our innovative application to offer clear, jargon-free instructions, error-checkings and real-time feedback, ensuring compliance without headache.

Q&A

Q1. How are you different?

We don't just dictate instructions; we guide you through every step of the process, ensuring clarity and real-time support as you implement the most crucial and effective security measures.

Q2. How can I contribute?

Our partnership involves ongoing engagement, agile-based meetings to tailor our roadmap to your specific needs, sharing wireframes and actively involving you in testing our MVP.

Q3. How is it beneficial for me?

We offer free access to our MVP for testing and significant discounts on the final product for our initial partners, as well as prioritise addressing the problems your organisation faces.

Q4. When will the product be ready?

Tailoring to the needs, feedback, and traction garnered from our partners and clients, the timeline for our MVP release may range from 3 to 6 months (no later than September 2024).
